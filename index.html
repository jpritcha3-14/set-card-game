Instructions

Click to select 3 cards that are either all the same or all different for each of their properties. The 4 properties are:

  1. Color
  2. Number (of symbols)
  3. Shape
  4. Shading

If your selection is not a valid set, an explanation will appear to the right of the game area. Click selected cards to deselect them. Sometimes no SETs will be present among the cards on board, in these cases the deck will be shuffled and 12 new cards will be delt
